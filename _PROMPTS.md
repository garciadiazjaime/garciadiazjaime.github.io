## The "Technical Fact-Checker" Prompt

"Please review the following blog post strictly for factual accuracy, technical consistency, and logical flow. Grammar and tone are already finalized, so do not suggest stylistic changes.

Review Criteria:

Fact-Check: Flag any claims, dates, statistics, or technical definitions that seem incorrect or outdated.

Logical Gaps: Identify any 'leaps in logic' where a conclusion is reached without enough supporting information.

Internal Consistency: Ensure that if a term is defined or a process is explained in the beginning, it remains consistent throughout the post.

Current Relevance: If any mentioned technologies, libraries, or tax laws have changed significantly as of 2026, please note the update.

Feedback Format:
Please do not rewrite the post. Instead, provide a bulleted list of 'Observations' or 'Corrections' citing the specific section and why it needs attention.
